不善于做总结或者复盘（其实是不做总结或复盘）是很大的一个弱点，所以错误总会一而再再而三的犯，随之而来的就会有挫败感，学习英语如此，工作生活中皆如此。

今天的生词
  • infinite (adj)： without limits in space or  time : the universe is infinite.
反义词: finite
副词：infinitely : very much : our new office building is infinitely better than the old one
名词：infinity: 1) a space or distance without limits or an end: the infinity of space
                       2) a number that is too large to be calculated : an infinity of possible solutions

  • albatross : 1) a very large white sea bird
                          2) an albatross ( around your neck) sth that causes problems for you and prevents you from succeeding : the project became a financial albatross for the city.

  • immense: extremely large : an immense palace
  • sustenance: 1)food that people, animals , or plants need to stay alive and healthy: the buffalo was the plains indian's main source of sustenance 
                             2) sth that gives you strength or hope
  • yield: 这个词多重意思，找时间好好消化
  • harness: to control and use the natural force or power of sth.  the XX river is harnessed for hydroelectric power
